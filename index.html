Status Saver for WhatsApp

Status Saver for WhatsApp allows users to view and save photo and video statuses from WhatsApp directly on their device.

Features

  • Save WhatsApp photo statuses
  • Save WhatsApp video statuses
  • Offline viewing
  • Simple and lightweight
